## BUG-010 — Pagination breaks after applying price filter

**Title:** Pagination breaks after applying price filter  
**Environment:** Chrome 124, Windows 11  
**Severity:** Major | **Priority:** Medium  

**Steps to Reproduce:**
1. Go to shop
2. Apply price filter: $20–$80
3. Navigate to page 2

**Expected Result:** Page 2 shows filtered products within $20–$80  
**Actual Result:** Price filter is cleared; page 2 shows all unfiltered products
