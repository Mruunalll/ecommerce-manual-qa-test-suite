## BUG-007 — Search does not return results for partial SKU match

**Title:** Search does not return results for partial SKU match  
**Environment:** All browsers  
**Severity:** Minor | **Priority:** Low  

**Steps to Reproduce:**
1. Know a product with SKU "SHOE-RD-L-001"
2. Search for "SHOE-RD"

**Expected Result:** Product appears in results  
**Actual Result:** No results found; full SKU works but partial match fails
