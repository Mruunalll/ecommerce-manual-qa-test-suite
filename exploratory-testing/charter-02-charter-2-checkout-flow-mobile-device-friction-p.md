# Charter 2: Checkout Flow — Mobile Device Friction Points  
**Mission:** Identify usability and functional issues in checkout on small screens  
**Area:** Checkout → Mobile (iPhone 14 Safari, Galaxy S23 Chrome)  
**Duration:** 90 minutes  

**Session Notes:**
- iPhone: keyboard hides CTA (BUG-004) ❌
- Galaxy: autofill suggestions overlap form labels — labels hidden behind autofill dropdown ⚠️
- iPhone: "Country" dropdown too small to tap accurately (touch target ~28px, min should be 44px) ❌
- Both devices: Stripe card field does not trigger numeric keyboard on mobile — text keyboard shown instead ❌
- Tablet (768px): Checkout form shows in two columns but second column partially cut off ❌

**Observations:**
- PayPal button on mobile opens new tab and doesn't redirect back correctly on iOS
- Coupon field not visible without scrolling — users may miss it
